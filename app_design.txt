User Interface and User Experience Design:

1. Login Screen: The app should have a login screen that allows users to log in using their email address or social media accounts.

2. Dashboard: The app should have a user dashboard that displays the user's account information, virtual currency balance, and campaign history.

3. Campaign Creation Screen: The app should have a campaign creation screen that allows users to create and manage their campaigns.

4. Video Selection Screen: The app should have a video selection screen that allows users to select videos to be viewed, for how long, and how many views.

5. Analytics Screen: The app should have an analytics screen that provides users with analytics on their campaigns, including the number of views, engagement rate, and conversion rate.

6. Payment Gateway Integration: The app should have a payment gateway integration that allows users to purchase campaigns using their credit/debit cards or other payment methods.

7. Social Sharing: The app should allow users to share their campaigns on social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.
