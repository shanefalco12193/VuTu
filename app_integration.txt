YouTube Video Integration:

1. The app should allow users to select a video that they want viewed.

2. The app should integrate with the YouTube API to retrieve video information and display it within the app.

3. The app should allow users to search for videos by keyword or channel.

4. The app should allow users to filter videos by category or popularity.

5. The app should allow users to rate and comment on videos.

6. The app should allow users to share videos on social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.
